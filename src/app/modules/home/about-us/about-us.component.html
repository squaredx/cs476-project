Why WorkMVRK?

           Running a small business is an exciting opportunity for a budding entrepreneur. The freedom that comes with running your own business is one of the main drives for small business owners. However, unlike working as an employee for a larger company, the onus is on a sole individual or small group of individuals to keep everything in order--product listings, backlog tracking, order tracking, inventory tracking, etc.. As a result the team will be overwhelmed with administrative tasks--taking away time that would normally be dedicated towards their regular business duties. The design of WorkMVRK is aiming to solve the problems that small business owners may concern.


How does WorkMVRK work?

           WorkMVRK is a web-app designed to relieve the aforementioned stresses that are associated with running a small business. The web-app will empower entrepreneurs to organize and plan orders, track inventory, and expense/income reports. Business owners will register through the web-app and enter in their business details. The user can then log orders, expenses, and inventory through the interface. Additionally a dashboard will be provided so that the user can see important information at-a-glance. WorkMVRK will be a Single Page Application implemented using an MVC software architecture and will be powered through the Angular JavaScript framework. The backend functionality will be provided through Firebase, Google's backend-as-a-service solution. Additional backend functionality will be provided through Cloud Functions--which are essentially JavaScript functions running on the backend.
